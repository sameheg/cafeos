# Compliance Requirements

This document outlines electronic invoicing and tax requirements per country.

## Saudi Arabia
- QR code must be printed on all invoices.
- Electronic invoice payload must follow ZATCA guidelines.

## United Arab Emirates
- VAT at 5% applies to most sales.
- E-invoices are accepted but QR codes are optional.

## Egypt
- E-invoice submission to the tax authority is mandatory.
- QR codes required for B2C invoices.
