# Responsive Manual Checklist

Use the following checklist to verify the POS and report pages at major breakpoints.

## Mobile (<576px)
- POS layout stacks vertically and sidebar is hidden.
- Nonessential columns remain hidden in sale and product sell reports.
- Buttons and form controls have at least 44px tap targets.
- Capture screenshot of POS and report tables.

## Tablet (≥768px)
- POS form and sidebar display in two columns.
- Report tables show additional columns like contact info.
- Verify 44px tap targets.
- Capture screenshot of POS and report tables.

## Desktop (≥992px)
- Full POS grid with sidebar visible.
- All report columns are displayed.
- Capture screenshot of POS and report tables.

Document screenshots or notes for each breakpoint above.
